🎶 Battle of Bands ft. Gemini Lyria

Where music meets AI — your campus, your sound, your crew.

Date
21 June 2026
Format
Live Music Night
Powered By
Nano Banana + Lyria
The Brief: Form a band. Make a poster. Drop a track. The room decides who wins.
  1. Form a team — 3 people max. Give your band a name.
  2. Open Nano Banana — generate your band poster: name, vibe, aesthetic.
  3. Open Lyria — write a short brief using Band Name / Campus Vibe / Your Beef / One Word That Defines Your Crew, then generate your anthem.
  4. Present to the room — poster on screen, track playing, 60 seconds.
  5. Room votes: This Slaps or This Flops.
🏆 National winning band takes home Google Pixel Buds 2a for the whole crew.
